ABOUT
-----------------------------------------
A cozy collection of 10 Japanese treats in cute pixel art style — from dango and taiyaki to sakura mochi and melonpan. Perfect for café menus, UI, or adding a kawaii touch to your game projects. Includes individual PNGs and a spritesheet for easy use.

Sprite size: 64×64 pixels each (transparent PNG)
File formats: PNG
Suggested Pixels Per Unit (PPU) in Unity: 64
Filter Mode: Point (No Filter)
Compression: None

-----------------------------------------
CONTENTS
-----------------------------------------
- Spritesheet (PNG) — 320x128px, 10×2 grid, 64×64 each
- 10 individual PNGs 
- This ReadMe.txt
